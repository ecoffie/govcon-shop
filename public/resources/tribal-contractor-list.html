About Tribal 8(a) Contractors

Native American-owned businesses can qualify for the SBA's 8(a) Business Development Program through tribal affiliation. These companies have unique advantages:

  • Sole-source contracts: Can receive sole-source contracts with no dollar ceiling (unlike other 8(a)s)
  • No competitive bid requirements: Federal agencies can award directly without competition
  • Joint ventures: Can form JVs with larger companies while maintaining 8(a) status
  • Community development: Profits often support tribal communities

Why Partner with Tribal Contractors?

  • Access to sole-source opportunities
  • Support set-aside requirements without competition
  • Build relationships with growing federal contractors
  • Contribute to tribal economic development

How to Find Tribal Contractors

The best sources for finding tribal 8(a) contractors include:

  • SBA Dynamic Small Business Search: Filter by "Tribal 8(a)"
  • NAICS code filtering: Search within your industry codes
  • Agency small business offices: Request lists of tribal contractors
  • Industry conferences: Attend tribal business conferences

Top Industries for Tribal Contractors

  • Construction and facilities management
  • IT services and cybersecurity
  • Professional services
  • Environmental services
  • Healthcare support
  • Logistics and transportation
